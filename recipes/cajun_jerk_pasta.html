Cajun Jerk Pasta

Indulge in a mouthwatering fusion of flavors with our Cajun Jerk Pasta, featuring tender, slow-braised oxtails that melt in your mouth. This savory dish is complemented by sautéed onions, vibrant bell peppers, and a rich, creamy sauce with just the right amount of spice. The bold Cajun and jerk seasonings come together to create a perfect balance of heat and flavor, making every bite an unforgettable experience. Served over al dente pasta, this dish is a true comfort food with a bold, Caribbean twist.

Ingredient

  • 2 lbs oxtails, trimmed
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 1 large onion, thinly sliced
  • 1 red bell pepper, sliced
  • 1 green bell pepper, sliced
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tablespoon Cajun seasoning
  • 1 tablespoon jerk seasoning
  • 1 teaspoon smoked paprika
  • 1/2 teaspoon cayenne pepper (optional, for extra heat)
  • 1 cup heavy cream
  • 1 cup chicken or beef broth
  • 1 can (14 oz) diced tomatoes (optional)
  • 12 oz fettuccine or pasta of choice
  • 1/4 cup Parmesan cheese, grated (optional, for garnish)
  • 2 green onions, chopped (for garnish)
  • Fresh parsley, chopped (for garnish)
  • Salt and pepper, to taste

Steps

  1. Prepare the Oxtails:
    • Season the oxtails with salt, pepper, Cajun seasoning, and jerk seasoning.
    • Heat olive oil in a large pot over medium heat. Sear the oxtails on all sides until browned, about 3-4 minutes per side. Remove and set aside.
  2. Cook the Vegetables:
    • In the same pot, add the sliced onions, red bell pepper, green bell pepper, and minced garlic. Sauté until softened and fragrant, about 5 minutes.
  3. Braise the Oxtails:
    • Return the oxtails to the pot. Add the chicken or beef broth and diced tomatoes (if using). Bring the mixture to a boil, then reduce the heat to low, cover, and simmer for 2-3 hours, or until the oxtails are tender and falling off the bone.
  4. Cook the Pasta:
    • While the oxtails are braising, cook your pasta according to package instructions until al dente. Drain and set aside.
  5. Make the Sauce:
    • Once the oxtails are tender, remove them from the pot. Add the heavy cream, smoked paprika, and cayenne pepper (if using) to the pot. Stir well and let the sauce simmer for 5-10 minutes to thicken.
  6. Shred the Oxtails:
    • Carefully shred the meat from the oxtails using a fork, discarding the bones. Return the shredded meat to the sauce.
  7. Combine:
    • Toss the cooked pasta into the pot with the sauce and braised oxtails, stirring to coat the pasta evenly with the rich, creamy mixture.
  8. Garnish and Serve:
    • Garnish with grated Parmesan cheese, chopped green onions, and fresh parsley. Serve hot and enjoy your Cajun Jerk Pasta with Braised Oxtails!
