King Caesar Salad

Enjoy the timeless flavors of a classic Caesar Salad, featuring crisp romaine lettuce tossed in a creamy, tangy dressing made from scratch. Topped with crunchy homemade croutons, freshly shaved Parmesan cheese, and a hint of lemon, this salad offers the perfect balance of textures and flavors. Perfect as a side dish or a light meal on its own.

Ingredients

    For the Salad:
  • 1 large head of romaine lettuce, chopped
  • 1/2 cup freshly shaved Parmesan cheese
  • 1 cup croutons (homemade or store-bought)
  • 1 lemon wedge (for garnish)

    • For the Dressing:
  • 1/2 cup mayonnaise or Greek yogurt
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tablespoon Dijon mustard
  • 1 tablespoon Worcestershire sauce
  • 1 tablespoon fresh lemon juice
  • 2 teaspoons anchovy paste (optional)
  • 1/4 cup freshly grated Parmesan cheese
  • Salt and pepper, to taste
  • 1/4 cup olive oil

Steps

  1. Prepare the Dressing:
    • In a medium bowl, whisk together mayonnaise (or Greek yogurt), minced garlic, Dijon mustard, Worcestershire sauce, lemon juice, and anchovy paste (if using).
    • Gradually whisk in the olive oil until the dressing is smooth and creamy. Stir in the grated Parmesan cheese, and season with salt and pepper to taste.
  2. Assemble the Salad:
    • In a large salad bowl, toss the chopped romaine lettuce with the Caesar dressing until evenly coated.
    • Add croutons and freshly shaved Parmesan cheese, and toss lightly.
  3. Garnish and Serve:
    • Serve the salad immediately, garnished with a lemon wedge for a fresh citrus finish.
